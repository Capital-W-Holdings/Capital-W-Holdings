About

Capital W Holdings represents the culmination of a decade spent at the intersection of technology, finance, and entrepreneurship.

As the founder and CEO of Deal Flow Xchange, I've built infrastructure that connects founders with capital across global markets. The private capital ecosystem is fragmented—my work is focused on creating the coordination layer that enables better matches between great founders and aligned investors.

My approach to building is defined by velocity. By leveraging AI as a development partner, I've consistently compressed traditional 6-month software projects into week-long builds. This isn't about cutting corners—it's about understanding how to use modern tools to their full potential.
